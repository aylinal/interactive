29 aug 1925

                                                       

           hello, poochums, i am writing to you
   from the shore of the titisee, where we
               are now sucking on chocolate ice. we’ll riting to you
   from the shore of the titisee, where we spend the night here and
                              tomorrow climb up feldberg. We had
                                             a wonderful time today swimming and lying in the heat.
                    i love you. very much. we passed through here.
       i love you. i adore you very much. my joy. my dear love.

                                                       V.
