letters to vera


                                                       19 jan 1925

           i love you. infinitely and inexpressibly.
   i’ve woken up in the middle of the night
    and here I am writing this. my love, my happiness.

                                                       V.

                                                       29 aug 1925

           hello, poochums, i am writing to you    from the shore of the titisee, where we                are now sucking on chocolate ice. we’ll riting to you    from the shore of the titisee, where we spend the night here and                               tomorrow climb up feldberg. We had                                              a wonderful time today swimming and lying in the heat.                     i love you. very much. we passed through here.

                                                       V.

                                                       30 jun 1926

           my little one, how are you doing? i             love you. the sky is starry now, with a warm                  wind blowing. the roses, fossilized by now, are still standing on my table. a whole          month! my little one, I am kissing you         all over. there was a letterlet today. what’s  that about the little monkey?

                                                       V.

                                                       8 nov 1925

           you came into my life – not as one                         comes to visit (you know, ‘not taking one’s hat off’) but as one comes to a               kingdom where all the rivers have been       waiting for your reflection, all the roads, for         your steps. fate wanted to correct its                                     mistake – as if it has asked      my forgiveness for all of its previous                    deceptions. so how can i leave you, my        fairy-tale, my sun?

                                                       V.
